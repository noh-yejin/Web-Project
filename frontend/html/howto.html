How to Use EAAI

✍️ Enter your thoughts freely in the journal section to begin your emotional reflection journey.

📅 Make sure to select the correct date before submitting your entry. This ensures your emotions are tracked accurately over time.

🤖 Click on the "Analyze Emotion" button. Our AI will interpret your diary entry and provide an emotional analysis.

📊 You can view the emotion results, extracted keywords, and personalized advice immediately after analysis.

📅 Use the Emotion Calendar to track your mood trends and reflections across different days.
